Our menu is updated according to the season. We have a range of children's and adult meals and seasonal dishes from soups to Shepherds Pie which are popular and sell fast!

We offer a range of sweet treats and puddings depending on the season and availability. Our home-made scones are popular and sell out fast! Delicious on their own or with jam.

Latte, Americano, Espresso or Cappuccino? the choice is yours. But... when you just want chocolate-comfort, our Luxury Hot Chocolate with whipped cream and marshmallow pieces hits the spot!.

Our range of all-day breakfasts are cooked to order. Breakfast baps are very popular, followed by the 5-item breakfast.
For a meat-free option we also do a Veggie Breakfast Special, and our new smashed avocado and egg on toast.
